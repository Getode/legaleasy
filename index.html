How can I protect my startup’s intellectual property?

Protecting your intellectual property (IP) is crucial for startups. We help you identify what needs protection—such as your brand name, logo, or unique product—and guide you through the process of securing trademarks, copyrights, or patents. We also assist with non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) to safeguard your ideas when working with partners or investors.

Do I need a lawyer to draft contracts for my business?

While you can draft contracts yourself, having a lawyer ensures your agreements are legally sound and tailored to your needs.
